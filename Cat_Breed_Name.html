Chartreux

The Chartreux might well be compared to a mime, silent but communicative and sometimes silly.

  • Amount of Shedding

  • General Health

  • Potential for Playfulness

  • Kid-Friendly

  • Friendly Toward Strangers

  • Easy to Groom

  • Pet Friendly

15 to 18 inches
